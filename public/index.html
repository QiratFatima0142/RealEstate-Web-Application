How it works

  1. 1

    Create your account

    Sign up with your email and a secure password. Your password is hashed with bcrypt before it ever touches the database.

  2. 2

    Record a purchase

    Add property details - name, area, total amount, purchase date, and an optional photo. Everything lands in a normalised MySQL table.

  3. 3

    Close a sale

    When you sell, log the sale total, amount received, and the next installment date. EstateEase tracks outstanding balances for you.

  4. 4

    Measure & optimise

    Your dashboard tallies portfolio value and revenue received, so you always know the state of your business.
